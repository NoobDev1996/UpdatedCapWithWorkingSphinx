About Us

Introducing the fiery and passionate sounds of Illusion, a melodic hardcore band that is quickly making a name for themselves in the underground music scene. Hailing from Wyoming, this band is comprised of four talented musicians who are all driven by a deep love for hardcore punk and metal music.

With their unrelenting passion for creating music that truly matters, Illusion is a band that is sure to leave a lasting impression on anyone who hears their music. Whether you're a die-hard fan of hardcore punk, or simply a lover of powerful and emotionally charged music, Illusion is a band that you won't want to miss.
